Cartesian Control (X-Y)

Up and down on the Joystick controls the Y-Axis position of the robot arm end-effector. Left and Right on the Joystick controls the X-Axis position of the robot arm end-effector

Cartesian Control (Z)

Up and down on the Joystick controls the Z-Axis position of the robot arm end-effector.

Pitch and Yaw Control

Up and down on the Joystick controls the Yaw of the robot arm end-effector. Left and Right on the Joystick controls the Pitch of the robot arm end-effector.

Roll Control

Up and down on the Joystick controls the Roll of the robot arm end-effector.
